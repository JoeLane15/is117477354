Cloud Servives

In this section I will be discussing the various types of cloud services.

Cloud services are becoming increasingly more popular for a number of reasons:

  • Supports Accessibilty: As the data is stored in the cloud, you can access youe data from nearly anywhere in the world. This eliminates the need to carry your usb on your person.

  • Offers Reliablity: Cloud services are also exceptionally reliable. They provide a service for hundreds and thousands of people, be it for their own personal use or professional. Most cloud services have staff on hand to answer questions and help to resolve any problems they may have.

  • Saves Money: Using cloud services is Capital expenditure free, as you pay as you go subscription to avail of the service. This significantly reduces the cost for a company as they don't have to buy all the high cost hardware. You also do not have the financial burden of maintaning the system, which is cost efficient.
