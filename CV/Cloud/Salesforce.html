Salesforce and Software as a Sale

About Salesforce:

Salesforce.com is a cloud computing company, founded in 1999 by Marc Benioff.It provides companies with an interface for case management and task management, and a system for automatically routing and escalating important events. The Salesforce customer portal provides customers the ability to track their own cases, includes a social networking plug-in that enables the user to join the conversation about their company on social networking websites, provides analytical tools and other services including email alert, Google search, and access to customers' entitlement and contracts

Software as a Sale:

Software as a Service or SaaS is a method of delivering an application over the internet, as a service. Webmail, bookmarking and photo sharing were the first applications for users. One of the advantages of SaaS is how fast they can get services up and running, SaaS implementation is fast and easy. Cloud computing allows companies to consume computing resources as a utility. Recent developments have helped make this a better option for many businesses than setting up and running their computing infrastructure in-house.
