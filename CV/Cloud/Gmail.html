Gmail and Communications as a Service

Gmail is a free email service developed by Google. Gmail was launched in 2007. It's users can access this service from anywhere in the world provided they have a device that can connect to the internet and an internet connection. As well as accessing their Gmail via the web,users can also avail of the apps for both iOS and Android devices.


Communications as a Service:

Communications as a Service (CaaS)is an outsourced enterprise communications solution that can be leased from a single vendor. These communications can vary from vice over IP to Instant Messaging such as Gmail. Google is responsible for all of Gamil's hardware and software management and offers guaranteed Quality of Service. Gmail offers flexibility and expandability that small and medium-sized business might not otherwise afford, allowing for the addition of devices, modes or coverage on demand.
